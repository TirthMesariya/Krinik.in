Refund Policy

General Policy

Krinik In aims to provide a fair and transparent experience for all our users. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued for transactions made on our platform. By using our services, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Refund Eligibility

  • Ineligible Transactions: Payments made for in-game purchases, entry fees for contests, and other digital assets are generally non-refundable. Refunds are only considered under specific conditions as outlined below.
  • Technical Issues: If a payment is unsuccessful or there are technical issues that result in an incomplete transaction, please contact us within 24 hours. We will work to either complete the transaction or issue a refund if deemed appropriate.

Specific Scenarios for Refunds

Refunds may be issued at our sole discretion under the following scenarios:

  • Duplicate Transactions: If you are charged twice for the same transaction, contact support within 24 hours. Upon verification, we will process a refund for the duplicate payment.
  • Cancelled Contests: If a contest is canceled by Krinik In due to technical issues or lack of participation, all entry fees for the canceled contest will be refunded to your account balance.
  • Unauthorized Payments: If you believe an unauthorized payment has been made on your account, report it immediately. Refund eligibility for unauthorized payments will be determined based on our investigation.

Process for Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@krinikin.in with the following information:

  • Username and registered email address.
  • Transaction ID or proof of payment..
  • Reason for requesting a refund.

Our team will review and respond to your request within 7 business days.

Refund Method

Approved refunds will generally be credited back to the original payment method within 7–10 business days, depending on your bank or payment provider.

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided for:

  • Any entry fees for contests completed without technical issues.
  • Situations where a user is found violating our Terms & Conditions, leading to account suspension or disqualification.
  • User change of mind or any dissatisfaction after contest results have been declared.

Changes to this Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of the revised policy
